My Top 5 Favorite Games of All Time

  1. Pokemon Soul Silver (2010)

    2. This is one of the first video games I ever played, and one I played a ton as a kid so it holds a special place in my heart. Still the best Pokemon game to date in my opinion.

  2. Chrono Trigger (1995)

    3. One of the greatest games of all time. The amazing soundtrack and character design by the late Akira Toriyama remains timeless. If you haven't played it yet you're missing out.

  3. Terraria (2011)

    4. One of the first PC games I played when I got a computer. It's a game I always come back to whether its by myself or with friends. I have almost 1000 hours on it and that number will only go up with the game's infinite replayability.

  4. Golden Sun (2001)

    5. Another classic RPG series on the Gameboy Advance. It has fun turn based combat and has puzzle solving akin to The Legend of Zelda.

  5. Monster Hunter 4 Ultimate (2015)

    6. Monster Hunter is probably my favorite game series because of its satisfying action combat. I've played most of the games but I think 4 Ultimate still remains my favorite. Played it a lot as a kid and I still go back to it a lot.
